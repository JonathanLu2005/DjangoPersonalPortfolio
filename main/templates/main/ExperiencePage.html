Working Experience

JP Morgan

Through the Social Mobility Foundation (SMF) I was able to undertake work experience at JP Morgan in August 2022 and July 2023.
With 2022 involving me shadowing business analysts and 2023 shadowing software engineers and learning how technology is used in the financial services.
I was able to gain insight on how to better prepare myself for applications (CVs, cover letters, assessment centres etc).
Alongside gain an insight on what the working world was like and what to do technically to be a competitive applicant (internships, spring weeks, learning languages and doing projects, software and tools to learn etc).

  • Utilised and enhanced my presentation skills
  • Able to adapt and work independently in an unfamiliar environment
  • An inquisitive mindset when exploring different aspects of technology

Travis Perkins

By applying locally in my area to various companies, I was able to undertake a week long work experience at Travis Perkins from March to April 2022.
This involved me shadowing different lines of technology used in the logistics industry: data science, cyber security, software engineering, project management etc.
I was able to learn and see how technology is used in logistics and also ask questions to learn more about what I should do to be proactive and prepare for University.

  • Conducted and communicated professionally
  • Learnt how to use Excel to analyse data
  • Networked with employees
